# 1. Product Overview

**Velara** is Vitalief's proprietary skills platform. It converts consultant methodology — currently distributed across individual practitioners, slide decks, and ad-hoc artifacts — into governed, versioned, deployable units called **skills**. Skills are the firm's intellectual property: reusable, auditable, and protected from unauthorized exposure.

The product brand is **Velara**. Vitalief is the parent company and operator. All product surfaces use the "Velara · A Vitalief Skills Platform" identity.

The platform serves two user populations with opposing needs. Internal users (Vitalief consultants) need access to the full methodology embedded in each skill. External users (clients) need to invoke skills and receive outputs without ever seeing the methodology, code, or instructions that produced them. The platform enforces this boundary at every layer.

Phase 1 is a one-month build by MA Technologies. It produces a working platform that can register, version, execute, and audit skills. Phase 2 is an ongoing authoring partnership. This PRD scopes Phase 1.

# 12. Out of Scope

- Sale of the platform or skill library as a standalone product to third parties
- Client self-service skill authoring
- Multi-tenant SaaS hosting for multiple consulting firms
- Vitalief-built model training or fine-tuning
- Replacement of client systems of record (CTMS, EDC, CRM) — the platform integrates, does not replace
- Full client-facing web portal (Phase 2)
